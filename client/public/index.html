How Feedia Works

Our platform makes food sharing simple, efficient, and rewarding for everyone involved.

Step 1

Donors List Food

Register and list surplus food with details, images, and pickup instructions.

Step 2

Pickup Services Collect

Drivers or volunteers collect and deliver food to designated locations.

Step 3

Beneficiaries Receive

Food is distributed to those in need, reducing waste and fighting hunger.
